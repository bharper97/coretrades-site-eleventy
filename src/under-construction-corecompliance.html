Platform Features

From automated compliance tracking to secure storage, Core Trades Document Management SaaS has everything you need to manage pre-employment and induction paperwork seamlessly.

Pre-Employment File Folders

Digitally store and organize resumes, offer letters, and onboarding forms in one centralized location for each employee.

Government & Safety Compliance

Track and store driver’s abstracts, WSIB certificates, and safety training records. Receive alerts before documents expire to maintain compliance.

Medical Documentation

Securely collect and manage pre-hire medical forms, immunization records, and fit-for-duty certificates with encrypted file storage.

Custom Workflows & Approvals

Create tailored induction workflows. Route documents for approval, track sign-offs, and log timestamps for audit-ready record-keeping.

Digital Signatures

Enable candidates and management to sign onboarding checklists, NDAs, and policy acknowledgments electronically—no printing required.

Automated Notifications

Configure reminders for expiring certifications, license renewals, and mandatory safety renewals. Keep your workforce always ready.
