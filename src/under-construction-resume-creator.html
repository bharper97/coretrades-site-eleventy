Resume Creator

Coming soon: a step-by-step tool to build a professional, trade-focused resume. From selecting your trade category to formatting your work experience, we’ll guide you through every section.

How It Will Work

1. Choose Your Trade: Select from categories like Welding, Electrical, Carpentry, Mining, Heavy Equipment Operation, etc.

2. Enter Your Details: Fill out contact info, certifications, work history, and skills. We’ll provide industry-specific prompts (e.g., safety certifications for Construction, Red Seal details for Electricians).

3. Pick a Template: We’ll offer trade-focused resume templates that highlight relevant skills—whether you’re a plumber showcasing pipefitting experience or a millwright listing machinery maintenance.

4. Format & Download: Preview your resume, export as PDF or Word, and get tips on customizing for each job application in the industrial trades space.

5. Additional Resources: Optional cover-letter builder, interview prep checklists, and downloadable “Jobsite Safety” quick-reference sheets to include with your application.
